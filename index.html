My name is Minas Marios Sotiriou, I am a Phd student in the department of Informatics at the Athens University of Economics and Business fortuned to be advised by Alkmini Sgouritsa.

I have completed the inter-institutional graduate program ALMA: "Algorithms, Logic, and discrete MAthematics" from the National and Kapodistrian Unversity of Athens, and the National Technical University of Athens.

I received my bachelor's degree from the departement of Informatics and Telecommunications from the National and Kapodistrian Unversity of Athens

I primarly work in Fair Division, but my other research interests include Mechanism Design especially in Cost Sharing games, Fair Division with strategic agents, Algorithms with Predictions, Mechanism Design with Predictions, and Fair Division with predictions.
